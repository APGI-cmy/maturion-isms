## What "Decommission Pending" Means

Effective immediately upon this directive:

1. **No new AIMC wiring work** may be authorised for the Roadmap module
2. **No CL-12d** sub-wave exists or will be created in the AIMC CEP
3. **No new waves** for Roadmap as an active development target
4. Roadmap remains in the repository as a **migration anchor** — its structure and cross-references guide MMM feature parity validation
5. Roadmap remains as a **traceability artifact** — all cross-references to Roadmap capabilities must be maintained until formal decommission is executed
6. Formal decommission is triggered when **MMM parity is confirmed** by foreman and CS2

## Decommission Trigger Conditions

The following conditions must ALL be satisfied before formal Roadmap decommission is authorised:

1. **MMM Parity Confirmed**: MMM (Maturion Maturity Module) delivers functional parity with all Roadmap capabilities as described in the MMM app description.
2. **CL-12c Complete**: Wave CL-12c deliverables fully GREEN (MMM AIMC wiring complete).
3. **Cross-Reference Audit**: All cross-references from other modules/governance documents to Roadmap have been reviewed and updated to reference MMM equivalents.
4. **No Knowledge Discarded**: LKIAC-001 §8 Principle 1 compliance confirmed — all valuable Roadmap assets verified as present in MMM before decommission.
5. **Deprecation Register Entry**: Formal Deprecation Register entry created per LKIAC-001 §8 Principle 3.
6. **CS2 Authorisation**: CS2 issues formal decommission sign-off.
