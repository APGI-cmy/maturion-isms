3. Apps provide context and workflow surfaces; they do not create separate Maturion brains.
4. Maturion may invoke only approved and registered capabilities within a bounded task context and must review specialist output before responding to the user.
5. Runtime Maturion does not inherit CS2, merge, builder, deployment, production-write, protected-file or governance-amendment authority from app context.
6. Agent-file-system changes remain protected and require exact CS2 authority, CodexAdvisor execution and independent IAA.
7. No QA-to-RED means no implementation build. Builders build only to GREEN against approved pre-build and QA obligations.
