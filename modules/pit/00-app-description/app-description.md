## Module Purpose

PIT (Penetration Intelligence Tool) is the threat intelligence and penetration testing management
module within the Maturion ISMS platform. It provides capabilities for threat intelligence feed
management, IOC (Indicator of Compromise) management, TTP (Tactics, Techniques & Procedures)
correlation, vulnerability tracking, and integration with the risk management and ISMS navigator
modules.
