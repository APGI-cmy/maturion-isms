## 1. Purpose

This artifact defines what evidence is required to accept Slice 2.1 as a specification/governance slice.

---

## 2. Required review evidence

Slice 2.1 requires evidence that reviewers checked:

- the scope declaration;
- App Description addendum;
- UX/wiring addendum;
- FRS addendum;
- TRS addendum;
- Architecture addendum;
- QA-to-Red baseline;
- traceability note;
- traceability matrix;
- IAA pre-brief contract;
- builder hold notice.

---

## 3. Required acceptance evidence

Acceptance must state:

- dual integrated and standalone journeys are specified;
- shared Maturion account model is preserved;
- PIT-only and full-bundle entitlement are specified;
- project, milestone, deliverable and task role families are acknowledged;
- host boundary is preserved;
- QA-to-Red exists before implementation;
- implementation remains prohibited until a later build slice.

---

## 4. Future implementation evidence

The future build slice must capture browser evidence for integrated entry, standalone entry, signup/account, entitlement, onboarding, role-aware navigation and host boundary behavior.

---

## 5. Non-completion notice

This evidence expectation does not claim PIT completion, Slice 2.1 build completion, Stage 12 completion, production readiness, release readiness, functional pass or handover completion.
