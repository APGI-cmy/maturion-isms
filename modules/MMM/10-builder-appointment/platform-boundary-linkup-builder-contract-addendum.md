# MMM Builder Contract Addendum - ISMS Platform Boundary Linkup

Status: pre-build boundary alignment
Authority: PR #1850 and paired ISMS/MMM platform-module boundary artifacts
Date: 2026-06-24

## Contract rule

Any builder appointed to MMM/ISMS linkup work is bound by the shared platform/module boundary strategy.

## Builder may build

Only after a separate governed build wave and QA-to-red exists, MMM builder may build:

- MMM-owned Maturity Roadmap runtime after approved ISMS handoff;
- MMM assessment, scoring, roadmap, evidence, approval, and workflow behavior explicitly scoped to MMM;
- MMM descriptor data consumed by ISMS.

## Builder may not build

MMM builder may not build or modify:

- ISMS public landing;
- ISMS modules overview;
- ISMS public free-assessment entry;
- ISMS marketing routes;
- subscription / checkout;
- authentication;
- onboarding;
- dashboard;
- entitlement/journey-state storage;
- PIT, Risk Management, RADAM / Systems Integration, or other module runtime.

## Evidence rule

Runtime evidence for future linkup work must prove the canonical ISMS-host journey and must not rely on cross-origin local storage as proof of continuity.

## Claim restriction

No builder may claim completion, production readiness, release readiness, or fully functional state from PR #1850 or the MMM boundary artifact alone.
