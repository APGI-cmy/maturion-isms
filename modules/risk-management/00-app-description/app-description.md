## Module Purpose

The Risk Management module is the risk register and risk assessment platform within the Maturion ISMS
platform. It provides capabilities for risk identification, threat and vulnerability assessment, risk
scoring and ranking, control effectiveness evaluation, risk treatment planning, and integration
with the maturity assessment and ISMS navigator modules.
